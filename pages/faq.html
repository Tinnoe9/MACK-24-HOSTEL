🏠 Accommodation

Yes, Mack 24 Hostel is a 100% female-only hostel. We accommodate female students, working-class spinsters, and NYSC members who are ladies.

We offer Single Rooms (private), Double Rooms (for 2 people), and Shared Rooms (up to 6 occupants). All rooms come with air conditioning, comfortable beds, and wardrobe space. Visit our Rooms page for full details.

Absolutely! We encourage prospective residents to visit and inspect the hostel before making any payment. Contact us to schedule a visit at a convenient time.

This depends on the room type. Some single rooms have en-suite bathrooms. Shared and double rooms have communal bathrooms that are cleaned regularly. Ask us during your visit about specific room configurations.

💳 Payment & Application

You can fill out our online application form on the Book a Room page, or contact us directly via WhatsApp or phone. Our team will respond within 24 hours to discuss availability and next steps.

Yes, a refundable caution fee is required at the time of check-in. This is fully refundable at the end of your stay, provided there is no damage to the room or property. Contact us for the current amount.

Yes! We occasionally offer referral bonuses and discounts for residents who refer friends. Subscribe to our newsletter or follow us on social media to stay updated on current deals and promotions.

📍 Location & Access

Mack 24 Hostel is located right behind UNILAG's 2nd Gate on Majaro Street, Onike, Yaba. It's approximately a 5-minute walk or drive to the university gate — one of the closest female hostels to UNILAG.

Yes! YABATECH is also within a short distance of the hostel — approximately a 5–10 minute drive or bus ride away, making it convenient for YABATECH students as well.

Our address is: 24 Majaro Street, Onike, Yaba, Lagos State. You can find us on Google Maps by searching "Mack 24 Hostel Yaba." Visit our Contact page for a live map.

⚡ Facilities & Rules

Yes! We operate a Generator + Inverter system to ensure 24/7 power supply for all residents. You'll never have to worry about studying or working in the dark.

As a female-only hostel, male visitors are only allowed in designated common/reception areas during visiting hours. Access to rooms and private floors is restricted to female residents only for the safety of all residents.

Yes, we welcome female NYSC corps members! Many of our residents are corpers serving in Lagos. Contact us to discuss availability during your service year.
