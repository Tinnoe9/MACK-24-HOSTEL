About Mack 24

More Than a Hostel.
It's Your Community.

Nestled in the heart of Onike, Yaba, Mack 24 Hostel has been the home away from home for hundreds of female students and working spinsters since 2017. We don't just offer a bed — we offer a secure, vibrant environment where you can study, grow, and thrive.

With proximity to the University of Lagos and Yaba College of Technology, every resource you need is within reach. Our team ensures your comfort, safety, and wellbeing are always the priority.
